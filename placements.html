Our placement policy

Transparent. Fair. Beneficial to every stakeholder.

The campus placement policy ensures recruitment at SEA Group of Institutions is transparent, fair and beneficial for all stakeholders — especially students and companies. It creates an atmosphere where recruiters and students interact freely and effectively.

The Placement Department facilitates and coordinates every placement activity, physical or virtual. It provides the platform for students to get trained and groomed for selection processes and organises the recruitment drives — while students enrol, prepare, participate and perform to earn their placement.

How placements work

Three stages to an offer.

01

Registration

Register with the Placement Department with academic records, skill sets and contact details — updates are shared with every registered student, digitally and manually.

02

Enrollment

When companies bring a job description, interested students study the role, package and location, then enrol in the drive through the prescribed format.

03

Selection

Meet the company's eligibility — academic criteria, no backlogs, strong communication — and appear for the selection process to earn the offer.
