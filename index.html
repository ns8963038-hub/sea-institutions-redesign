About S.E.A. Education Trust

Rooted in values. Built for what's next.

The South East Asian Education Trust was established in 2000 with one objective — bringing academic excellence within reach of every section of society.

The campus, with its extensively landscaped gardens, is widely acknowledged as one of the most beautiful in Bengaluru — harmoniously combining buildings and open spaces, with lecture and performance theatres, studios, laboratories and campus-wide computer facilities running the latest hardware and software.
