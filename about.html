S.E.A. Education Trust

A trust built on the belief that education transforms.

Established in 2000, the South East Asian Education Trust brings quality education, integrated personality development and research-led learning to every section of society.

25+Years of excellence

Our story

One of Bengaluru's most beautiful campuses.

The Trust focuses on imparting quality education, disciplined and integrated personality development, research, and the management skills needed to face global challenges.

The campus, with its extensively landscaped gardens, harmoniously combines buildings and open spaces. World-class facilities sit alongside heritage-protected buildings and state-of-the-art teaching and research spaces — lecture and performance theatres, tutorial spaces, studios and laboratories, with campus-wide computer facilities running the latest general and subject-specific software.

  • Founded by Sri A. Krishnappa, whose work in education began in 1972
  • Twelve institutions spanning school to doctoral research
  • A learning philosophy of Do, Feel, Learn, Reflect and Be

What guides us

Vision & mission.

Our vision

We aspire to empower learners with meaningful experiences that will enable them to become reflective individuals who will discover themselves.

Our mission

To provide a progressive international education in a diverse, learner-centred community — where learners develop the knowledge, skills and values to evolve into successful leaders who create meaningful change in a global society.

Leadership

Messages from the people who built SEA.

Sri A. Krishnappa

Founder, S.E.A.E.T. · Ex-Minister, Government of Karnataka

“We believe that education is the bedrock of all transformation and progress. In pursuit of that belief we entered the field of education in 1972 with the Sri Venugopalaswamy Educational Trust, and in 2000 the South East Asian Education Trust was formed.”

“The thrust here is to blend the modern and the traditional thoroughly. Our teaching-learning technique is based on Do, Feel, Learn, Reflect and Be — children learn through exploration and experimentation, critically examining things and suggesting alternatives. Field trips, educational tours and visits to historical and cultural places raise their aspirations.”

“Mere intellectual growth is not enough: the refinement of the soul — Atma Samskriti — is needed. Our students will continue to draw inspiration from the institution's motto: Arise! Awake! And stop not till the goal is reached.”

Mrs. Manjula A Krishnappa

Chairman, S.E.A. Group of Institutions

“Success is not a one-shot process — it is the result of continuous improvement after each failure. Failure while working for a good cause is better than success in working for a wrong one.”

“Over the years, the S.E.A. Education Trust has built a special position in private higher education. With its distinctive culture it provides a clearly student-centred environment for exploring existing technical knowledge and gaining new learning at the leading edges of technology.”

“The aim is to make our students autonomous and socially responsible human beings — capable of self-direction and ready for employment the day they graduate. At every stage there will be opportunities to expand your boundaries, platforms for collaboration, and recognition for those who strive to excel.”

Prof. (Dr.) K. Viyyanna Rao

Director, SEA Group of Institutions

Former Vice-Chancellor of Acharya Nagarjuna University and in-charge of Krishna & Vikrama Simhapuri Universities, Prof. Rao brings 35 years of teaching experience in commerce and management studies, having guided 10 Ph.D. and 14 M.Phil. scholars.

He has authored 10 books across financial management, industrial economy and banking, published 121 research papers in national and international journals, and designed courses for IGNOU's M.Com and MBA programmes.

His recognitions include the Best Research Paper Award from the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (2002 & 2005), the Best Teacher Award from the Government of Andhra Pradesh (2009), and the Outstanding Excellence in Academic Leadership Award from the Nexus Eclat Honours Society, Maryland, USA (2012).
