Life at SEA

A campus that gives back your energy.

Landscaped gardens, serious laboratories, warm hostels and honest food — the SEA campus in K R Puram is built for whole students, not just syllabi.

Infrastructure Digital campus Hostels Sports

Infrastructure

Built to a standard, kept like a garden.

The campus meets every infrastructure requirement specified by its governing institutions — and then some. Rich green environments, properly built internal roadways and street lighting frame well-designed, well-ventilated classrooms.

Beautifully planted gardens let students refresh mind and body between classes, while training sessions keep the laboratories current in both hardware and software.

Central library

Books, magazines, journals and newspapers with separate reading rooms, a computer zone, microfilming space, documentation centre and audio-visual room.

Computer laboratories

Network-enabled labs connected through a centralised server room, with regular hardware and software training sessions.

Auditorium

A dedicated auditorium for major events, alongside lecture and performance theatres across campus.

Gym & fitness

A campus gym designed to help students burn off surplus energy and stay match-fit.

Sports courts

Football and cricket fields plus basketball, volleyball and badminton courts.

Guest house

An on-campus guest house that accommodates visiting faculty and guests.

Hostels & accommodation

A second home, seriously kept.

  • Separate hostels for boys and girls inside the college campus
  • Comfortable, well-furnished rooms with 24-hour security and housekeeping
  • Campus cafeteria serving a variety of cuisines
  • Round-the-clock medical facilities
  • A separate hostel for international students

Ragging is strictly prohibited inside and outside the campus and hostels, per Section 116 of the Karnataka Education Act 1983. Anti-ragging committees and squads of senior faculty and wardens act on any incident, with action as per law.

Sports

Play is part of the timetable.

The SEA multipurpose indoor stadium and grounds host both indoor and outdoor games — football and cricket fields, basketball, volleyball and badminton courts, and a campus gym.

Sports and co-curricular activities play a vital role in shaping a complete student: the Trust actively supports yoga, meditation, inter-college fests, sports and science clubs, seminars, quizzing and debates.

Safe & secure

A diverse community, well looked after.

Students from across India and abroad make up the SEA campus community. A dedicated security chief oversees the entire campus — including the residential areas — with round-the-clock security, guarded entry and exit points at the main building and hostels, and an access protocol that keeps daily life hassle-free.
