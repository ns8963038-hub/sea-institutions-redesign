Eligibility & categories

Everything you need to know.

SEA Group believes in moving with the times and is often a torchbearer in adopting new techniques in education — the admission process included. Students and parents can review syllabus and placement details for every course on this site and choose accordingly; if you still need help, approach us directly and we will guide you along.

The admission process is transparent: if the documents and mark sheets meet the requirements and the student is selected for the applied course, we intimate you at the earliest by email or telephone. Once you receive news of your admission, the course fee must be paid before the due date, failing which the admission is considered void.

SEA College strongly believes in the fair treatment of everyone who applies. Here's wishing you good luck — and hoping you will soon be part of the South East Asian family.

SEA guides admitted students toward education loans from banks and recognised lenders. Typical education loans cover tuition, living and other expenses, offer repayment periods of 5–15 years, and include a moratorium (repayment holiday) of 6–12 months after the course ends.

Documents lenders usually ask of the applicant:

  • Aadhaar / PAN card
  • Class X and XII mark sheets
  • University admission letter
  • Salary slip, if employed

Co-applicant documents: Aadhaar, PAN, residence proof, 6-month bank statement, ITR / Form 16, income proof, and an assets & liabilities brief when offering collateral.

The Trust also plans and extends scholarships under merit and merit-cum-means categories, and coordinates schemes including GOI and National Scholarships, Jindal, community-based endowments, free-ships, SC/ST scholarships from the Social Welfare Board and alumni awards. Contact the admissions office for the current year's options.

The process for international students mirrors the standard one — the differences are the documents submitted and the visa steps that follow selection.

  • Apply online or offline (common to all students); selected students are intimated.
  • Part of the tuition fee is paid at this stage, based on the intimated amount.
  • The college then issues details for visa processing.
  • Submit all required documents and complete the tuition fee before the due date.

Documents for international admissions: mark sheets of 10th and 12th (or equivalent) — plus the UG degree mark sheet for PG applicants; an eligibility certificate from a relevant Indian university; conduct/character and transfer certificates; copies of a valid passport and student visa or residential permit; an HIV clearance certificate; and ten passport-size and ten stamp-size photographs.

Where the earlier medium of instruction was not English, an intensive six-month English course runs alongside the chosen stream. Write to seaeduinfo@seaedu.ac.in and our counsellors will guide you through the whole process.

International and NRI students seeking admission through the management process can contact the Admission Cell at the campus directly or apply through the website form — the admission office takes up further processing.

Selection is purely on academic merit and aptitude. No entrance tests are required; the college conducts an interview and assessment before granting admission.

Sample eligibility — B.E.: a pass in PUC or 10+2 equivalent with a minimum of 45% aggregate (40% for SC/ST and OBC) in Physics and Mathematics along with Biology, Chemistry, Computer Science or Electronics.
